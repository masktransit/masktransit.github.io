Learn About Us

Everyone should wear a mask

But unfortunately, not everyone is aware of this. We're trying to get the word out, and help the most vulnerable members of New York City get their hands on masks they can use to stop the spread of SARS-CoV-2.

The Centers for Disease Control (CDC) recommendations have changed

See our guide on mask use for COVID-19

that we're distributing with our masks. Includes info on wearing, taking off, and cleaning your mask. Available in English and Spanish. You can also see some important points below!

01

Silent Spreaders

Not everyone who is infected with the SARS-CoV-2 virus feels sick, and sometimes it takes time for people to have symptoms. However, people can still spread the virus to others.

02

Masks Help!

Studies show that masks may help reduce the amount of virus breathed in, but importantly, they also help stop the spread of the virus from people who already have it.

03

Old Rules Still Apply!

Wearing a mask can help slow the spread, but it's still important to practice social distancing, use good handwashing techniques, and to make sure that you're not touching your face!
